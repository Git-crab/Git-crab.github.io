About

Drawing inspiration from "Through New Eyes" by James B. Jordan, Through a Fresh Lens is a Christian blog that offers a distinctive viewpoint on entertainment from a Christian worldview. In today's media-saturated world, it's all too easy to be passive media consumers; however, the blog's goal is to encourage readers to approach entertainment with a critical eye. The blog explores various topics such as movies, TV shows, music, books, and more. Ultimately, the aim of Through a Fresh Lens is to help readers engage with entertainment in a positive and meaningful way while also maintaining their Christian principles and beliefs.
